The target audience for this site

The audience for this site are the people for whom it is important to stay informed of the important things that happen around them, whether it is about the weather or society, and based on that information they usually make decisions.

Personas

Megan Lee

Megan is 32 years old and is a mother of three children and is usually very busy, as she has many matters to attend outside the home with her children, it is important for her to check some news on the internet and have an overview of how her day will be in order to take care of her and her family.

Oscar Gomez

Oscar is 48 years old and he loves the outdoors, he likes to go out and his time and income allow it since he is very successful in his business and earns a good amount of money. Whenever he plans to go out, he first looks up what the weather will be like in the places he is going to visit to be prepared.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Oscar received a call from his friends telling him about a meeting in the national park, Oscar noticed that the day was cloudy and decided to check theweathernewstoday.com on his laptop to see if it was going to rain.

Scenario 2

One of Megan's children has an activity at school, in the morning Olivia decides to check on her cell phone if the day will be sunny and tells her family that it is important to use sunscreen

Scenario 3

Oscar is planning to leave the city to visit his parents, it is winter and he wants to know if it will snow this weekend, so he checks on the internet if there will be snow on the road.

Scenario 4

Megan is planning a party in the backyard of her house for the birthday of one of her children, she wants to make sure that the weather does not ruin the party, so she checks on the internet if it will rain for the birthday.

Scenario 5

Oscar has family near the beach, recently there have been storms where his family lives and he constantly checks the news on the internet to be aware of the situation
