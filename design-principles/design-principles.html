Visual Hierarchy

TED

https://www.ted.com/

The first thing we see on this site is this invitation, you can feel that the company is interested in you becoming a member to have a better experience on their site, their intention is to draw your attention to this part, that's why they made the Join today button prominent by using red color.

PARC: Proximity

Church News

https://www.thechurchnews.com/

On this site the types of news are grouped close to each other, for example in the Latest section or the Featured section we can see how these elements of the page are grouped, there is little space between them, in this way the site makes us understand that these elements have a relationship.

PARC: Contrast

Slack

https://slack.com/

In different sections of the site the background color is a bit different and in each section there is a good contrast in the colors of the text and the background, which makes the text easy to read and also blends in with other content.
